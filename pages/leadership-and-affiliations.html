Leadership & Professional Affiliations

From SHALIpedia, the personal encyclopedia

Shalini has founded and led organizations focused on equity in STEM, LGBTQ+ inclusion, and professional development for underrepresented communities. This page documents those leadership roles and the external organizations she has been affiliated with. See also Technical Skills and Community Involvement.

Founded Organizations

ColorStack Vanderbilt Chapter

Shalini founded the Vanderbilt chapter of ColorStack, a national community connecting Black and Latinx students in computer science. Duration: 2023–2025.

Latinx & Queer Affinity Group

Founded the Latinx & Queer Affinity Group at Vanderbilt (2022–2025), creating space for community and advocacy at the intersection of Latinx and LGBTQ+ identities.
