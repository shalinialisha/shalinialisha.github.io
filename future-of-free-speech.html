The Future of Free Speech Project, housed at the Vanderbilt Data Science Institute, explores the intersection of technology, content moderation, and democratic discourse. Shalini built a full-stack Streamlit application integrating Hugging Face transformer models to generate real-time counter-narratives to hate speech. This work required deep engagement with NLP and ML skills and was presented at industry symposiums including OpenAI and Microsoft.
